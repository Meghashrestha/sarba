OUR INTERIOR

1. General Consultation

We have regular General Consultation service each day by experienced General physicians from Kathmandu Medical college.  General Consultation from General physician or Internal medicine covers general health check-ups, Preventive Health Check up, Fever and infectious disease, Headache, Gastro-intestinal disease, Diarrhea and loose motion, abdomen Pain, Soar throat, Cough and chest infection, Bronchial Asthma, Allergies, Urinary tract condition and urinary track infection, Diabetes, Thyroid disorders, High blood pressure and many more. Our general physician also conducts regular trainings on all major non communicable diseases.

2. Gynecological Consultation:

We offer gynecological and Obstetric service by highly qualified gynecologist. Gynecological consultation includes general gynecological checkups, checkup for pregnant women (Monitoring during pregnancy), lactating mothers, family planning measures consultation and fertility consultation as well. 

3. Dermatological Consultation:

We have proven ourselves in the field od dermatology by our experienced dermatologist. We can treat almost all kind of skin problems. Apart from skin diseases one can consult about venereal diseases (Sexually transmitted diseases) as well.

 
4. Pediatrician consultation:

We offer pediatric consultation for children by our experienced doctors from Kanti Children hospital. Regular visit from young, energetic and friendly pediatrician always pleases our children during treatment. Our pediatrician always keeps in mind that, child should be kept safer from unnecessary exposure from drugs. Regular monitoring of physical and mental growth of children is also essential, hence we offer such service to schools as well.

5. Cardiology Consultation:

We offer cardiological consultation weekly from experienced and highly skilled cardiologist at our clinic.

6. Orthopedic Consultation:

We have recently extended orthopedic consultation service by our experienced doctor from Grandy hospital. 

7. Physiotherapy Clinic:

We have extended our dedicated  Physiotherapy service separately from the main clinic block. We provide, physiotherapy service between 8 am to 7 pm every day.

8. Psychiatry Consultation and Counselling:

Neuro-Psychiatry service is regularly provided by our experienced Neuro-psychiatric consultants in our clinic. Our psychiatrists are well experienced in dealing with exactly these depressive and anxiety conditions, not just with medications but with proper counseling as well. We also offer psychological trainings for employees of small, medium or large companies to cope up with stress.

10. Laboratory Service:

11. Dental Service:

Sarwanidan has been serving its local community with same zeal and commitment since many years in the filed of dental health. We also serve the schools of Katmandu to fortify the health of school kids.

We have wide range of pharmaceutical products with home delivery service around ratopul area. We have been ordering the medicines for those who are looking for medicine around the Kathmandu valley. We also offer consultation on medicine to those who are consuming many medicines for different diseases. 

13. Home call service
1. Physiotherapy service:

Our experienced, young and talented physiotherapist serve our patients all around the Kathmandu. We are best with home service since long time. Recently we have extended our home service all around the Kathmandu. We have named this service as “Meet the Physios”
