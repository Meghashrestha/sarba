Who We Are

SarwanidanClinic

Sarwanidan Clinic, Popularly known as Bharat ko Clinic was established by Bharat Maharjan in year 1990. Sarwanidan is registered polyclinic under Department of Ministry of health, serving the local community of Ratopul and periphery since last three decades. The polyclinic is supporting the local community on immunization, Dressings, General OPD, Dental, Laboratory services, along with pharmacy. Addition to all the existing services, the polyclinic is recently expended it service with physiotherapy and psychiatry service in same locality. The clinic has been maintaining its legacy at the vicinity for its personalized health care.
